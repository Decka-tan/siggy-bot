## 6. EVENT RULES & GUIDELINES

### GENERAL EVENT RULES (Announced August 22, 2025)

#### **For Gamers:**
- Make sure in-game name matches Discord name
- This ensures recognition and avoids confusion

#### **For Hosts:**
- After each event, send top 3 winners using Discord @mentions
- Use Discord tag, NOT display name or ID number
- Example: @kash_060 (not Kash(❖,❖) | event manager)

#### **Winner Rewards:**
- All top three winners get **100 XP each**
- Winners announced on 🎪┇events channel

#### **Disqualification Rules (Chess - August 24, 2025):**
- 1st and 2nd place winners were disqualified because Discord usernames didn't match in-game names
- Failed to properly identify themselves after winning
- Appeared as bots, leading to automatic disqualification
- **Rule:** All participants must ensure Discord name matches in-game username
- Must tag host to confirm identity when winning
- Failure = disqualification, prize awarded to next eligible participant

### SPECIAL EVENT-SPECIFIC RULES

#### **Karaoke Night:**
- FCFS (First Come First Served)
- Show up early for spots
- Limited participants
- Wait in line, raise arm when it's your turn
- Hosts give mic to speak

#### **Chess Tournament:**
- Registration opens 1 hour before event
- Don't wait till last minute
- Once time's up, that's it

#### **Poker:**
- Sometimes FCFS (first 50 only announced)
- No cash on the line
- Pride and glory only
- Right to talk smack until next round

#### **Puzzle Games:**
- Open link on laptop or phone
- Shuffle the puzzle
- First to finish wins
